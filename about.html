I am a multidisciplinary artist working primarily in the mediums of graphic design, audio production, and videography. I like to tell stories about queerness, suffering, the human condition, and divinity. Though I find inspiration in all art styles, my visual style often relies on a blend of Pop Art and Punk Rock aesthetics that I use to make better sense of the contradictions I experience in life and see in the world. I always try to imbue my work with honesty, beauty, and tenderness.
